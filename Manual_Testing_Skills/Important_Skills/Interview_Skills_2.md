
# 🥇 1. Clear Structure (7 mins)

## ✅ Drill: “3-Line Answer”

Pick ANY question:

> “Tell me about a challenge”

Answer in ONLY 3 lines:

- Problem:
- What I did:
- Result:

---

### 🧪 Example

> Problem: No requirements in SKU project  
> Action: Talked to stakeholders, created test cases  
> Result: Found 55 bugs, smooth release


# 🥈 2. Ownership Mindset (5 mins)

## ✅ Drill: “Replace weak words”

Take 3 sentences daily:

❌ “We did testing”  
👉 ✅ “I took ownership of testing”

❌ “Team faced issue”  
👉 ✅ “I identified the issue”

---

## 📈 Measure improvement

- Day 1: You say “we” a lot ❌
- Day 7: Naturally say “I did / I decided” ✅

👉 Goal: **your role becomes clear**

---

# 🥉 3. Impact Awareness (5 mins)

## ✅ Drill: “Add impact to everything”

Take 3 actions and add impact:

> “I created test cases”  
> 👉 “I created test cases which helped ensure better coverage”

> “I automated flow”  
> 👉 “which reduced manual effort”

---

## 📈 Measure improvement

- Day 1: You forget to mention result ❌
- Day 7: Every answer ends with impact automatically ✅

👉 Goal: **no answer without result**

---
